Step-by-Step Guide: Set Up TikTok Shop Store

Learn how to launch your store on TikTok Shop using Printful, from initial requirements to promotion and order management.

Initial Requirements

  • Have a personal TikTok account
  • Valid identification documents (ID, passport)
  • Tax information (for invoicing)
  • Bank account to receive payments
  • Printful: free account (printful.com)

Basic TikTok Shop Setup

  1. Switch to TikTok Business account:
    • In the TikTok app: Profile ‚Üí ‚ãÆ (Menu) ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Switch to Business Account
    • Select category: "E-commerce" or "Online store"
  2. Register on TikTok Seller Center:
    • Go to seller.tiktok.com
    • Fill out the form with personal data, tax information, identification documents, and bank account
    • Wait for verification (1-3 business days)
  3. Set Up Shipping Methods:
    • In Seller Center: Settings ‚Üí Shipping
    • Select: "Shipping by Seller" (Printful will handle shipping)

Integrate Printful (Dropshipping)

  1. Install Printful app:
    • In TikTok Seller Center: Apps ‚Üí Search "Printful" ‚Üí Install
    • Connect your Printful account
  2. Sync Products:
    • In Printful: Dashboard ‚Üí Stores ‚Üí TikTok Shop ‚Üí Connect
    • Select compatible products: Apparel (t-shirts, hoodies)
  3. Set Prices:
    • Printful will show base cost (e.g.: t-shirt = $12)
    • Set your sale price (recommended: base cost + 50-70%)
    • Example: T-shirt cost $12 ‚Üí Sale price $19-$21

Publish and Sell

  1. Publish products:
    • In TikTok Seller Center: Products ‚Üí Product list ‚Üí "Publish"
    • Check that they appear on your profile (@yourusername ‚Üí Store)
  2. Basic Promotion:
    • Create videos showcasing the products
    • Use hashtags: #TikTokShop #YourBrand
    • In videos: Add direct link to the product
    • Content examples: "How this design is printed", "Shipping pack for customers", "Live customization"
  3. Manage Orders:
    • When you receive a sale:
      1. TikTok notifies you
      2. Printful automatically receives the order
      3. Printful prints and ships (3-7 days)
      4. TikTok deposits earnings to your bank (minus commissions)

Key Tips

  • Check product compatibility on Printful
  • Start with 5-10 popular designs
  • Shipping price: Printful calculates it automatically
  • Available countries: Spain, Mexico, USA, United Kingdom
  • Customer service: Respond to messages in <24h
