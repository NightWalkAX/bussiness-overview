Step-by-Step Guide: Open an Etsy Store

Learn how to open your Etsy store, connect Printful, and start selling personalized products.

Initial Requirements

  • Email address
  • Identification document
  • Credit/debit card
  • Bank account or PayPal

Step-by-Step Setup

  1. Register an Account:
    • Go to etsy.com
    • Click "Open a shop" (top right corner)
    • Create an account with email or Google/Facebook
  2. Choose a Shop Name:
    • Find a unique name (e.g.: "LunaCrafts")
    • Check availability
    • Tip: Use keywords (art, handmade, personalized)
  3. Set Up Payments:
    • Go to "Finances" → "Payment methods"
    • Choose:
      • Bank account: Enter IBAN and details
      • PayPal (recommended): Connect your account
    • Add a card to pay Etsy fees
  4. Set Up Shipping:
    • In "Settings" → "Shipping options"
    • Select:
      • Countries you ship to (Spain, EU, Worldwide)
      • Delivery times (e.g.: 3-5 business days)
      • Costs: Free or calculated by weight
  5. Create Your First Product:
    • Click "Add a product"
    • Fill in:
      • Photos: At least 5 images (white background)
      • Title: E.g.: "Personalized Mug - Unique Gift"
      • Description: Materials, measurements, and process
      • Price: $15-$25 (consider fees)
      • Tags: gift, handmade, personalized mug
  6. Shop Policies:
    • Create in "Settings" → "Policies":
    • Returns (e.g.: 14 days), Privacy, Production times
  7. Publish Your Shop:
    • Complete the verification checklist
    • Click "Open shop"

Connect Printful (Dropshipping)

  1. In Etsy: Go to "Apps" → Search "Printful"
  2. Connect your Printful account
  3. Sync products:
    • In Printful: Design your product
    • Publish to Etsy (created automatically)

Important Costs

  • Listing: $0.20 USD per product
  • Sales commission: 6.5% of total
  • Payment processing: 4% + $0.25 USD
  • Transaction fee: 5% if using Etsy Payments

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with a maximum of 10 products
  • Use keywords in titles (e.g.: "gift for couples")
  • Reply to messages within 24 hours
  • Promote on Instagram/Pinterest with a link to Etsy
⚠️ Note: Etsy may suspend accounts without identity verification. Upload your document in "Verify your account".
