Set Up Payouts in Printful

Learn how to receive your Printful earnings safely and quickly, meeting all requirements.

Requirements

  • Printful account (free)
  • Identification document (ID, passport)
  • Bank details (IBAN or account number)

Steps to Receive Payouts

  1. Go to Payout Settings:
    • Log in to printful.com
    • Click your name → "Billing"
  2. Add Payout Method:
    • Select "Payout Methods"
    • Choose "Bank Transfer"
    • Fill in:
      • Account holder's name (must match your document)
      • Bank country
      • IBAN (full account number)
      • SWIFT/BIC code (optional)
  3. Identity Verification:
    • Printful will request:
      • Photo of your document (front and back)
      • Selfie holding the document
    • Upload files in "Verification" → "Identity"
  4. Confirm Bank Account:
    • Printful will make a small deposit (less than $1) to your account
    • In 1-3 days, check your bank and note the exact amount
    • Enter that amount in Printful to verify
  5. Set Payout Frequency:
    • Go to "Payout Schedule"
    • Choose:
      • Automatic: Printful pays you every Monday
      • Manual: You request payouts whenever you want

Key Tips

  • Use a personal bank account (not business at first)
  • Names must match exactly (document, bank, and Printful)
  • First payouts take 7-10 days
  • Support: support@printful.com for issues
⚠️ Important: Printful does NOT charge to send you money, but your bank may charge fees for international transfers.
